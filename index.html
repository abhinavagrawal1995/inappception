Phase One : Before publishing

Before the app is published on the store, we use hard-coded data by searching and aggregating popular apps for roughly 30-50 locations. The app will NOT work properly in other locations, hence it is itself region specific as of now. Target areas for this phase are locations for popular campuses, and internship concentrated areas like bangalore.


Phase Two : First quarter

After the launch, we can expect downloads in the locations for which we have added data, these people (students and interns) will be from different hometowns, but in the same location for that time period. Once they're back to their respective hometowns (summer/winter break), we use their app lists, and suggestions to create datasets for those locations.
